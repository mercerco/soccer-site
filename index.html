Practice & Game Details

[Practice] Preseason Practice – Sept 19

Focus: Meeting teammates, basic ball comfort
Activities
  • Warm-up: Animal Tag (5 min) — Run like animals while staying in the field box.
  • Dribbling: Red Light/Green Light (8 min) — Dribble on green, stop on red, slow on yellow.
  • Dribbling: Follow the Leader (8 min) — Copy coach turns, stops, and pace.
  • Shooting: Monster in Goal (8 min) — Score past the silly monster coach.
  • Scrimmage: 3v3 free play (10 min) — Small game—lots of touches.
  • Cheer & stickers (2–3 min) — End happy and proud.
Required Equipment
  • Balls (1/player)
  • Cones
  • Goal

[Practice] Week 1 Practice – Sept 26 (Game Sept 27)

Focus: Starting/stopping, control
Activities
  • Warm-up: Lava Islands (5 min) — Run/jump between cone 'islands'.
  • Dribbling: Red Light/Green Light + Yellow (8 min) — Add slow 'yellow' speed control.
  • Dribbling: Treasure Hunt (8 min) — Stop ball at each 'treasure' cone.
  • Shooting: Cannon Fire (8 min) — Rapid shots into the goal.
  • Scrimmage (10 min) — Let them play.
  • Cheer (2 min) — Quick huddle and cheer.
Required Equipment
  • Balls
  • Cones
  • Goal

[Game] Game 1 – Sept 27

Go have fun! Lots of subs and smiles.

[Practice] Week 2 Practice – Oct 3 (Game Oct 4)

Focus: Awareness, keeping ball close
Activities
  • Warm-up: Freeze Tag (5 min) — Taggers freeze, teammates unfreeze.
  • Dribbling: Sharks and Minnows (8 min) — Protect ball from sharks.
  • Dribbling: Knock-the-Cone (8 min) — Dribble close and tap cones down.
  • Shooting: Kick the Coach (8 min) — Pass/shot to coach's feet.
  • Scrimmage (10 min) — Free play.
  • Cheer (2 min) — Team chant.
Required Equipment
  • Balls
  • Cones
  • Goal

[Game] Game 2 – Oct 4

Go have fun! Rotate often.

[Break] Fall Break (No Practice/Game)

No activities Oct 6–12.

  • Scrimmage (10 min) — Free play.

[Practice] Week 4 Practice – Oct 24 (Game Oct 25)

Focus: Intro to passing to a partner
Activities
  • Warm-up: Chase the Coach (5 min) — Tag the coach safely.
  • Passing: Gate Passing (8 min) — Pass through cone gates.
  • Passing: Kick the Coach (8 min) — Aim to coach's feet.
  • Shooting: Cannon Fire (8 min) — Quick finishing.
  • Scrimmage (10 min) — Free play.
  • Cheer (2 min) — Team chant.

[Game] Game – Oct 25

[Practice] Week 5 Practice – Oct 31 (Game Nov 1) 🎃 Halloween Fun

Focus: Dribbling, passing, and shooting with a Halloween twist
Activities
  • Pumpkin Patch Relay (5 min) — Each player dribbles their 'pumpkin' (ball) to a cone in the patch and back.
  • Trick-or-Treat Houses (8 min) — Cones are 'houses.' Stop ball, shout 'Trick or treat!' then go to the next.
  • Ghost Gates (8 min) — Pass through cone 'ghosts' to scare them away.
  • Monster Knockdown (8 min) — Shoot to knock down 'monster' cones. Coach can be a silly zombie goalie.
  • Costume Match Scrimmage (10 min) — Play as witches, bats, or pumpkins. Team 'pumpkins' vs 'bats' if using pinnies.
  • 'BOO!' Team Huddle (2 min) — Big 'BOO!' and Halloween sticker/glow stick.
Required Equipment
  • Balls (1/player)
  • Cones (houses, gates, monsters)
  • Goal
  • Optional Halloween props (pumpkin bucket, spider rings, glow sticks, silly hat)

[Game] Game – Nov 1

Go have fun!
